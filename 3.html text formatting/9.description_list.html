Coffee
a hot drink made from the roasted and ground seeds (coffee beans) of a tropical shrub.
Milk
an opaque white fluid rich in fat and protein, secreted by female mammals for the nourishment of their young.
Tea
a hot drink made by infusing the dried crushed leaves of the tea plant in boiling water.
Smoothie
a thick, smooth drink of fresh fruit pureed with milk, yogurt, or ice cream.
Juice
the liquid obtained from or present in fruit or vegetables.
