Dr. Tetsu Nakamura

Humanitarian who devoted his lift to Afghanistan.

Here's a time line of Dr. Tetsu Nakamura's life:

  • 1946 - Born in Fukuoka, Japan.
  • 1973 - Graduated from Kyushu University School of Medicine.
  • 1984 - Arrived in the region as a volunteer with the Japan Overseas Christian Medical Cooperative Service at the Mission Hospital.
  • 1991 - Opened three clinics to provide medical service in Nangarhar Province, Afghanistan.
  • 2000 - A drought hit the region. Nakamura stated about this situation: "One irrigation canal will do more good than 100 doctors."
  • 2003 - Started building an irrigation canal in the Khewa District (Kuz Kunar) of Nangarhar Province, the Marwarid Canal.
  • 2019 - Fatally shot in a targeted killing in Jalalabad, Afghanistan.
